Cubing Beginner Method

I first learnt how to solve a Rubik's cube using the beginner method from JPerm (Dylan Wang), or if you like, Cubehead (Milan Struyf with Matty Inaba) shared a tutorial using the same method recently. Below is quick reference of this method, but if you are just getting started, then you should watch their videos as they explain the steps and rotation logic rather than using cube notation.

This method uses repetitions of the Righty alg (R U R' U') or Lefty alg (L' U' L U) in several stages, with the Sune and Niklas algorithms to orientate the last layer, edges and corners. It is also worth learning some of the faster techniques e.g. skipping daisy and solving white cross in down layer, or some finger tricks.

With practice, you should find you have the learnt to solve a cube within 2 to 3 minutes. If you want to progress to faster times and are ready to learn some more algorithms, then see this beginner CFOP 2-look method.

How to solve a Rubik's cube

STEP 1 - FIRST LAYER WHITE CROSS

The objective of the 1st step is to create a white cross. Hold cube with white face upwards. If white edge is in top or bottom layer, align edge color with its side-face center and rotate that face to bring it into position in the top layer. If edge is in side layer, move it into the bottom and rotate it away so you can re-align the edge, then solve as before. If an edge is flipped, you need to rotate the white layer so the edge can be rotated into the target slot.

STEP 2 - FIRST LAYER CORNERS

The objective of the 2nd step is to solve the first layer. Hold cube with white cross downwards. Rotate top layer so white corner piece is over target slot, between the other colour centers. If the corner has white facing right, use righty alg. If white is facing left use the lefty alg. If white is facing up, use 3 repetitions of the righty alg. If corner is already in the wrong slot, hold it on the right and use righty alg to take it out of the slot, and then re-insert it in as before. Repeat to solve for each corner slot.

STEP 3 - MIDDLE LAYER EDGES

The objective of the 3rd step is to solve the second layer. Hold cube facing a non-yellow edge that need to solve. Align this edge with center of the side colour and check which side the other colour matches. Then use algorithm to rotate edge away, take corner piece out, setup corner-edge pair and re-insert. Repeat this until all edge pieces in the second layer are solved. If only yellow pieces facing upward, then swap one of these in a target edge using same technique.


U' (L' U' L U) y' (R U R' U')

U (R U R' U') y (L' U' L U)
RIGHT CASE
STEP 4 - LAST LAYER YELLOW CROSS

The objective of the 4th step is to create a yellow cross. Hold cube with yellow face as upward layer and rotate it, so pattern is either dot, hook (back/left), or (horizontal) line. Use algorithm and repeat until have a yellow cross (3x for dot, 2x for hook, 1x for line).


F (R U R' U') F'
DOT

F (R U R' U') F'
HOOK

F (R U R' U') F'
LINE
STEP 5 - LAST LAYER EDGES ORIENTATION

The objective of the 5th step is to orientate the edges in the third layer. Rotate the upward layer to align as many edges with their center colour (always match 2). If matching edges are next to each other, hold cube so they are back/right, then use Sune algorithm to solve. If they are across from each other, hold them in any pattern, then use Sune algorithm to setup adjacent pair, and repeat for this case. You may need an upper layer (U) rotation to re-align edges with their center.


(R U R') U (R U2 R')
HOLD SOLVED EDGES BACK/RIGHT
STEP 6 - LAST LAYER CORNER PERMUTATION

The objective of the 6th step is to position the corners in the third layer. If 1 corner is in correct slot between its other colour centers, hold it in the front/right. If 0 are correct, hold any corner in the front/right. Use Niklas algorithm to position the corners (observe how both sides are rotated up, then down, intermixed with a u-face rotation) and repeat until all four corners are aligned and the edges are over their correct center. You may need an upper layer (U) rotation to re-align edges with their center.


(U R U' L') (U R' U' L)
HOLD SOLVED CORNER FRONT/RIGHT
STEP 7 - LAST LAYER CORNER ORIENTATION

The objective of the 7th step is to solve the corners in the third layer, there by solving the cube. Hold cube flipped, with yellow face downward, with an unsolved corner in front/right position. Repeatedly use the righty alg until the corner is solved. If need to solve other corners, rotate bottom layer (D) to bring the unsolved corner into the front/right position, and repeat the process. Remember to do all 4 moves of the righty alg and rotate the bottom layer not the cube - if you do either of these by mistake, you'll likely need to start the solve the again.


(R U R' U')
HOLD UNSOLVED CORNER FRONT/RIGHT
