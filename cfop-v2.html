CFOP Beginner Method

The site is built to make it easier to practice (i.e. replay) the specific moves per case whilst learning CFOP. All examples and notes are based on the excellent tutorials for Beginner CFOP from CubeHead.

See these links to his YouTube videos: Intuitive CROSS | Intuitive F2L | 2-look OLL | 2-look PLL
